How important are the votes of the pan-democratic legislators?

Reported by: Roy Tang, Jessie Pang and Zoe Hu

The failure of "keep the critical vote appeal"

Despite the political storm of disqualifying the Legco by-election candidates in the past few weeks, Hong Kong has just passed one of the most quietest election with low-turnout rate. Candidates of the pan-democratic camp have been using “keep the critical votes” as emergency appeal to call for more voters. Yet, they failed to win all of the four seats back, as polling result suggests that there’s a shrink of its vote share.
